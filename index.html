ABOUT US

In the world of growing smartphones. The ability to capture iconic pictures with smartphone cameras has reached to a new level.

Photogram, A platform by AITIAN'S , makes this come true. Our platfrom focuses on enriching students' interest in photography and teaches how to convert moments into long-lasting memories.

Not only this we also provied services to professional photographers In order to increase work opportunity for them. Our platform also conducts different activities for it's members round the year at the college, intercollegiate and national level . There are various events such as Photowalks around different places of the city, Photography Exhibitions on various genres and Training sessions for the club members.
